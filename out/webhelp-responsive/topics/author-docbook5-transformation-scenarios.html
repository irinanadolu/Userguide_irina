DocBook Transformation Scenarios

Built-in transformation scenarios allow you to transform DocBook documents to a variety of outputs, such as WebHelp, PDF, HTML, HTML Chunk, XHTML, XHTML Chunk, and EPUB. Oxygen XML Author also includes a DocBook 5.1 transformation scenario for Assembly documents. All of them are listed in the DocBook 4 and DocBook 5 sections in the Configure Transformation Scenario(s) dialog box.
