Reserving a Floating License

There are instances where you might want to reserve or lock a floating license. For example, you could lock a floating license if you want to use your floating license offline while traveling.

To reserve/lock a floating license, follow these steps:

  1. Select Lock floating license from the Help menu.
  2. Click OK.
    Your floating license is now locked. You can use the same action to unlock the license or you can contact your system administrator to unlock it.
